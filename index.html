How txtpod works

How is txtpod different from text to speech apps?

txtpod does not keep you in a new player. It creates a private podcast feed so your narrated articles show up in the podcast app you already use.

How is txtpod different from read later apps?

txtpod is focused on listening. It converts articles to audio and sends them to your podcast app rather than building another reading list.

Is txtpod a podcast player?

txtpod is not a full podcast player. It generates a personal podcast feed of your narrated articles that you play inside txtpod or in your favorite podcast player like Apple Podcasts, Overcast, Pocket Casts, and more.

What does txtpod do?

txtpod saves links of articles and converts it into natural audio to listen later, and publishes each narration to your private podcast feed.

Which podcast apps can I use?

You can subscribe to your private feed in Apple Podcasts, Overcast, Pocket Casts, Castro, and most podcast players that support private RSS feeds.

How many voices and languages are supported?

Choose from 30+ voice styles across 20+ languages. You can set a default and change per episode.

Can txtpod translate articles?

Yes, txtpod can translate automatically so you can listen in your preferred language.

Does txtpod work with Instapaper, Readwise, or Matter?

Yes. You can save directly or sync with tools like Instapaper, Readwise, or Matter to pull in saved links.

Is my feed private?

Each account has a private RSS feed meant only for your personal use. You can share your link with others to subscribe to your podcast, however they cannot find it unles you share it.

Can I change voice, speed, or language per episode?

Yes. Pick a default voice and speed, then override them for any individual article. You can also select a different language for translation.

How do I add the feed to Apple Podcasts?

Copy your private feed URL from txtpod and paste it into Apple Podcasts using Add Show by URL. The episodes will download like any other podcast. This works in all podcast players that support private RSS feeds.

How about Overcast or Pocket Casts?

Use Add URL or Add Private Feed in your player. After subscribing, new narrations appear automatically.

Does txtpod support PDFs or newsletters?

Links to public web pages work best. PDF and email newsletter support varies by formatting, it all depends if is avaialble on a website link. However, you cannot upload directly PDFs to txtpod.

Can I re-render an episode with another voice?

You can create a new narration with a different voice or settings and keep both versions or replace the original. You just have to save and process the link again.

Do you offer summaries?

txtpod can generate optional AI summaries to review the main points before you listen.

What about paywalled articles?

txtpod works with publicly accessible pages. Whatever you can read, you can listen to with txtpod.

Is my data private and can I listen offline?

Your private feed is unique to your account. Episodes can be downloaded in txtpod or in your podcast app for offline listening.
